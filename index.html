How Grades Are Calculated?

This is how your grades are calculated:

This is how your results are calculated for example we have 7 subjects:

  • Subject 1: A
  • Subject 2: B+
  • Subject 3: O
  • Subject 4: A
  • Subject 5: B
  • Subject 6: C
  • Subject 7: A+

To calculate the SGPA, we assign the following points based on the grading system:

  • O = 10
  • A+ = 9
  • A = 8
  • B+ = 7
  • B = 6
  • C = 5
  • D = 4
  • F = 0
  • AB = 0

Calculation Steps:

  1. Step 1 : **Convert Grades to Points**: Each grade is converted to its corresponding point value.
  2. Step 2 : **Calculate Total Points**: Add the points for all subjects (each subject is multiplied by 3 for credits):
    • Subject 1: A = 8 points (3 credits) → 8 * 3 = 24 points
    • Subject 2: B+ = 7 points (3 credits) → 7 * 3 = 21 points
    • Subject 3: O = 10 points (3 credits) → 10 * 3 = 30 points
    • Subject 4: A = 8 points (3 credits) → 8 * 3 = 24 points
    • Subject 5: B = 6 points (3 credits) → 6 * 3 = 18 points
    • Subject 6: C = 5 points (3 credits) → 5 * 3 = 15 points
    • Subject 7: A+ = 9 points (3 credits) → 9 * 3 = 27 points
    Total Points = 24 + 21 + 30 + 24 + 18 + 15 + 27 = 169 points

**Calculate Total Credits**:

Assume each subject has equal credits (e.g., 3 credits each):

  • Total Credits = 3 (for Subject 1) + 3 (for Subject 2) + 3 (for Subject 3) + 3 (for Subject 4) + 3 (for Subject 5) + 3 (for Subject 6) + 3 (for Subject 7) = 21 credits

**Calculate SGPA**: Divide the total points by the total credits:

SGPA = Total Points / Total Credits = 169 / 21 ≈ 8.05

Thus, the calculated SGPA for the student is 8.05.

About website

This website was created to help students understand their grades and calculate their SGPA easily. The idea came to me when I noticed how challenging it was for students to keep track of their academic performance. I have also encountered issues while calculating SGPA, which led me to decide, "Why don't we create a website for this?" Although the website is functional, I recognize that it is not my best work yet, and I am committed to putting in more effort to improve it.

I would like to extend my heartfelt thanks to my friends who guided and motivated me throughout this journey. Without their support, this project would not have been possible. Their names are Sai, Vasu, Hemanth, and Sudhakar. They also assisted me in structuring the elements of the website, and their encouragement was invaluable in bringing this project to life.

A special thanks to Keerthi Mam for helping me understand how to calculate grades. She explained the process using a sample result, which clarified how it was done and significantly contributed to my understanding.

I hope this website serves as a useful tool for students and helps them achieve their academic goals! I will put in more effort in my upcoming projects and will try to upload results for different subjects, along with their corresponding credits, if possible. Thank you!
